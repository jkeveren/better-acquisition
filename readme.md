#### Enchanted Book
![](readme-images/enchantment_book.png)

Ingredients:
- Book
- 3 * Diamond
- 4 * Lapis Lazuli
- Other item that depends on target enchantment as documented below.

| Looting | Gold Block
| Mending | Emerald Block
| Power | Piston
| Protection | Shield
| Respiration | Glass Bottle
| Sharpness | Quartz Block
| Silk Touch | Cobweb
| Thorns | Cactus
| Unbreaking | Obsidian
